* Study the theoretical foundations and gain hands-on experience in implementing modern cloud infrastructure and optimized global data distribution
* Master computing automation, flexible server and container deployments, seamless data migration strategies, and enterprise disaster recovery planning.

### Week 4 Achievements:

* Mastered core cloud infrastructure architectures (RDS, DynamoDB, ElastiCache, Auto Scaling, Route 53, CloudFront), establishing a strong technical foundation to build highly available and cost-optimized web solutions.

* Gained structural competency in simplified container packaging and light-tier server administration via Lightsail, enabling efficient management of testing environments with predictable, fixed monthly expenses.

* Successfully executed comprehensive system migration workflows from local hypervisors to the cloud, covering both compute layers (VM Import/Export) and database instances (DMS) without causing production service disruption.

* Acquired advanced capabilities in enterprise risk management through AWS Elastic Disaster Recovery, securing continuous data protection and ensuring rapid business continuity recovery within a few minutes during critical failures.
